Privacy

R&P Music is designed for kids and families. We keep things simple.

What we collect

Nothing. No email, no name, no age, no account required to use the app. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising cookies.

Where your data lives

Profiles, hearted songs, and settings are stored only in your browser on your own device. We never see them. They never leave your device.

If you email us

When you write to readandplaymusicapp@gmail.com, we only use your message to reply. We don't share it with anyone.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you're a parent and have questions, please email us.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, this page will be updated.
